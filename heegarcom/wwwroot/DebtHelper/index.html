The Requirement

Every consumer bankruptcy has the same two mandatory courses.

Your client cannot file — or be discharged — without them, and both must come from a provider approved by the Executive Office for U.S. Trustees. DebtHelper is approved for both. And for clients who shouldn't file at all, there's still a path.

Pre-Filing Credit Counseling

Required within the 180 days before a client is eligible to file.

11 U.S.C. § 109(h)

Pre-Discharge Debtor Education

The personal financial management course required before discharge is granted.

11 U.S.C. § 111 / § 727
